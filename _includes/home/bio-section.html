Shengdong Zhao

I am a Full Professor in the School of Creative Media and the Department of Computer Science at the City University of Hong Kong. I was previously an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the National University of Singapore. I received my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Toronto, my master's degree from the UC Berkeley School of Information Management & Systems, and a dual bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Biology from Linfield College, Oregon. I founded the Synteraction Lab (formerly NUS-HCI Lab) in January 2009. My research focuses on innovative interface tools and applications that improve daily life, including Draco, which was named Best iPad App of the Year 2016. My work has been published in top HCI venues including CHI, UIST, UbiComp, CSCW, IUI, and ToCHI. I have served as Paper Co-Chair for CHI 2019, 2020, and UIST 2025, regularly participate in program committees of leading HCI conferences, and have worked as a senior consultant for Huawei Consumer Business Group.
