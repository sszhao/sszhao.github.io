On April 8th, 2026, we had the pleasure of hosting Dr. Tony Tang from the Singapore Management University (SMU), where he directs the [RICELab](http://ricelab.github.io/) (Rethinking Interaction, Collaboration and Engagement) research group. Prof. Luo Yuhan and two of our students joined us for a wonderful lunch at CityU's Bistro Lodge.

Tony's research spans human-AI interaction, mixed reality interfaces, ubiquitous computing, and AI/robot-supported activity — making for rich common ground with our own work at the Synteraction Lab. Over lunch, we had an energizing discussion in which Tony shared his hands-on experiences with Claude Code and AI development. It was both thought-provoking and inspirational.

It's always a delight to connect with fellow HCI researchers in the region, and we look forward to future collaborations and exchanges. Stay tuned!
