We are excited to share a new initiative we have started at Synteraction Lab — gathering together for a group lunch every Thursday. This week, we headed to Jinhui Restaurant in Nanshan Village, where we enjoyed a wonderful spread of local cuisine in a warm and lively atmosphere.

These Thursday lunches bring together PhD and master's students from across the lab, offering a relaxed setting to catch up, share ideas, and simply enjoy each other's company outside the research environment. It is a lovely way to build connections and explore the local lifestyle here as a group.

We look forward to making this a regular tradition and discovering more great spots together in the weeks to come!
