We are happy to share that, thanks to the initiative of Rui and Yingyu, our Synteraction Lab weekly running and eating tradition is back in full swing!

On the evening of April 9th, lab members gathered for a refreshing run at the Joint Sports Center, followed by a wonderful dinner at Nanshan Village's noodle shop. It was a perfect blend of exercise and camaraderie — exactly the kind of team bonding that keeps our lab spirit strong.

Everyone is welcome to join us for future sessions. Whether you're looking for some outdoor exercise or simply want to hang out with the team over a good meal, we'd love to have you!
