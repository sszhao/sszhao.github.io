Prof. Shengdong Zhao and
Prof. Mark Billinghurst Dine
in Adelaide

Professors Zhao and Billinghurst sharing insights over dinner in the heart of Adelaide, Australia.

P

rof. Shengdong Zhao and renowned AR/VR researcher Prof. Mark Billinghurst recently shared dinner in Adelaide, Australia. The meeting brought together two prominent figures in Human-Computer Interaction to discuss the evolving landscape of mixed reality and collaborative computing.

The conversation spanned several hours, touching upon the future of seamless interaction and the integration of artificial intelligence with augmented environments. Adelaide, known for its vibrant academic community and technological hubs, provided a serene backdrop for this intellectual exchange.

"The intersection of physical and digital worlds is no longer a research frontier, but a daily reality we must architect with care."

Prof. Billinghurst's pioneering work in the Empathic Computing Laboratory continues to influence how we perceive connection through technology, a vision that resonates deeply with Prof. Zhao's research on interactive systems and intelligent interfaces.
